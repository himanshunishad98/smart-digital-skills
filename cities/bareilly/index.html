FAQ About Computer Classes in Bareilly

Everything parents ask before enrolling — answered clearly.

❓ My child in Rajendra Nagar has no prior coding experience. Is this program suitable?
+
Yes, our course starts from the absolute basics of block coding in Scratch, making it extremely easy and engaging for complete beginners.
❓ What is the duration of the SkillNest digital skills program for Bareilly students?
+
The foundational cohort is completed in 6 weeks, with classes scheduled in flexible evening or weekend slots to fit around homework and school routines.
❓ Do students get a certificate at the end of the program?
+
Yes. Every student who builds their digital portfolio and submits projects receives a verified certificate of accomplishment from SkillNest.
❓ Is individual mentorship available during the live classes?
+
Yes, our small group sizing ensures the tutor interacts with each student, helping them debug Scratch bugs or spreadsheet equations in real time.
❓ Are there batch options for beginners in Bareilly?
+
Yes, we group students by skill readiness so that absolute beginners start with mouse operations and typing posture before moving to logic coding.
❓ How do parents track student progress?

We share weekly reports on WhatsApp and invite parents to a live digital portfolio showcase during the final week of classes.
❓ Can my child join if they are in Class 5 or Class 9?

Yes! While primarily tailored for Class 6-8, motivated Class 5 or Class 9 students are welcome after a quick digital readiness assessment.
