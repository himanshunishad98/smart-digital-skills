# About SkillNest (Smart Digital Skills)

## The Story of SkillNest
SkillNest was founded in Durg / Bhilai Nagar, Chhattisgarh, in 2024 to address a growing issue: children were spending hours in front of screens, but their digital capability remained extremely low. Most kids could swipe on smartphones but could not touch-type on a physical keyboard, organize files, write coding logic, build spreadsheets, or browse the web securely.

Traditional computer coaching institutes focused on memorizing definitions from outdated textbooks for school tests. To solve this, SkillNest designed a 100% practical, project-based online learning ecosystem. Today, we have trained over 1,000+ school students across India, building real digital competence and safety awareness.
