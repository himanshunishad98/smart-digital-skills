# SkillNest Company Profile

## Executive Summary
SkillNest (also known as Smart Digital Skills) is a premier practical, project-based educational platform offering live online computer, coding, and AI classes for school students, primarily focusing on Middle School (Class 6–8, Ages 10–14) in India. Established in 2024 by founder Aanandita Uplopwar, SkillNest's primary mission is to replace passive screen time with real-world digital skills that improve academic productivity and foster future-ready tech confidence.

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Middle school students (Class 6-8, Ages 10-14)
- **Secondary:** Elementary students (Class 3-5, Ages 8-10) and High school students (Class 9-10, Ages 14-16)
- **B2B Customers:** School administrators, CSR sponsors, and teacher coordinators

## Educational Approach
SkillNest implements a 100% practical, project-based learning model aligned with the guidelines of India's National Education Policy (NEP 2020). Standard cohort batches are capped at 5–10 students to enable individual attention, real-time screen-sharing debugging, and personalized instruction.
