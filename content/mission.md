# SkillNest Mission, Vision & Pedagogy

## 1. Our Mission
Our mission is to replace passive screen time with real-world digital capability. We empower kids (primarily Class 6–8) to shift from consumers of tech to active, confident creators who use computers to learn, design, build, and organize.

## 2. Our Vision
We envision a future where every school student in India, regardless of geography, is equipped with practical digital competencies—touch typing, computational thinking, data layout logic, and cyber hygiene—mandated for the modern digital economy.

## 3. NEP 2020 Mandates
India's National Education Policy 2020 (NEP 2020) outlines computational thinking, data literacy, coding, and internet safety as essential core subjects starting from Class 6. SkillNest directly bridges the gap between traditional theory-heavy IT textbooks and practical hands-on capability:
- **Practical Labs:** Bridging school IT lab constraints with interactive home-based screen sharing.
- **Problem Solving:** Integrating mathematics (geometry angles, Cartesian grids) directly with visual block coding projects.
- **Safety First:** Instilling digital safety hygiene to shield students from online fraud and gaming scams.

## 4. Pedagogical Pillars
- **100% Hands-On:** Every class is interactive, requiring students to code, type, layout, or prompt live on their own screens.
- **Project-Oriented:** Evaluation is based on real-world artifacts (projects, presentation decks, data spreadsheets, custom Scratch games).
- **Engagement First:** Using gamified typing contests, coding debug challenges, and collaborative reviews to keep cohorts excited.
- **Micro-Cohorts (5-10 kids):** Small batch sizes allow instructors to view screens, identify errors, and guide students step-by-step.
