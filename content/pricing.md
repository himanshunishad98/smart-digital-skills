# SkillNest Pricing & Billing Policy

## 1. Contact for Pricing Model
SkillNest operates on an inquiry-based pricing model rather than displaying standard prices online. Because every child starts with different levels of keyboard confidence and digital literacy, we customize our learning plans. 

Detailed program fee structures, discounts, and installment plans are shared directly and transparently with parents during our free 1-on-1 trial and demo booking session.

## 2. Free Trial & Assessment Session
- **Format:** 1-on-1 online live session.
- **Duration:** 30–45 minutes.
- **Components:** Basic typing speed assessment, computer navigation test, logic quiz, and customized pathway recommendation.
- **Cost:** 100% Free. No credit card required.
- **Booking Link:** [Book Free Demo](https://skillnest.co.in/contact/book-demo.html)

## 3. What is Included in Program Fees?
When a student enrolls in a SkillNest 6-week cohort, the program fee covers:
- **Live Instruction:** 12 live online interactive sessions.
- **Micro-Batch Size:** Batches capped at 5–10 students.
- **Learning Resources:** Complete access to printable worksheets, typing guides, and Scratch template code.
- **Projects & Review:** Individual review of student-submitted games, spreadsheets, and slides.
- **Certification:** Digital Certificate of Completion upon finishing all project submissions.

## 4. Refund & Cancellation Policy
- **Demo Trial:** Parents can experience a full class before making a payment decision.
- **Refund Guarantee:** If a parent is unsatisfied after the first class of the paid cohort, a full refund can be requested, no questions asked.
- **Contact Email:** admin@skillnest.co.in
