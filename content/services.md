# SkillNest Services & Programs Catalog

SkillNest offers interactive 6-week online cohorts designed to teach essential computer, coding, and AI skills to middle schoolers:

## 1. Computer Basics & File Management
- **Description:** Teaches foundational digital literacy, operating system navigation, task management, shortcut keys (such as copying/pasting and switching active apps), and clean folder structuring.
- **Key Outcome:** Shifting students from complete device beginners to confident, independent users who can navigate folders and manage school files.

## 2. Touch Typing Mastery
- **Description:** Focuses on correct keyboard hand posture, home-row finger placement, touch typing drills, and muscle memory training.
- **Key Outcome:** Helping students reach typing speeds of 40+ WPM with 95%+ accuracy without looking down at the keyboard.

## 3. Visual Block Coding (Scratch)
- **Description:** Introduction to computational thinking concepts including sequential logic, loop iterations, conditional statements, variables, and coordinate spacing.
- **Key Outcome:** Students design and build their own functional arcade games on the Scratch platform.

## 4. Generative AI Skills
- **Description:** Introduces kids to safe, productive generative AI tools, prompt templates (using Role, Task, Context, and Output limits), and cyber boundaries.
- **Key Outcome:** Ability to leverage AI safely as an academic study buddy and design assistant.

## 5. Microsoft Excel & Spreadsheets
- **Description:** Teaches data organization, basic formulas (addition, subtraction, multiplication, averages), cell references, and automatic column/pie chart formatting.
- **Key Outcome:** Ability to organize laboratory experiment data and build structured math spreadsheets.

## 6. Microsoft PowerPoint & presentation Design
- **Description:** Covers slide master templates, typography layouts, image integrations, presentation outline pacing, and Canva visual designs.
- **Key Outcome:** Designing professional slides and gaining confidence in public presentation speech layouts.

## 7. Cyber Safety & Internet Hygiene
- **Description:** Instruction on password security metrics, phishing identification tricks, cookie configurations, browser safety filters, and managing digital footprints.
- **Key Outcome:** Building responsible digital citizens who navigate the web safely and avoid online gaming scams.

## 8. Productivity Tools & Cloud Workspace
- **Description:** Teaches Google Workspace navigation, cloud file directories, layout margins, indexes, and document sharing setups.
- **Key Outcome:** Equipping students with the layout and formatting skills needed for high school and college coursework.

## Institutional & B2B Services
- **School Syllabus Alignment:** Setting up practical curricula for school computer labs.
- **Teacher Training Workshops:** Empowering IT teachers to instruct practical, project-based topics.
- **CSR Corporate Programs:** Partnering with corporations to fund digital literacy programs in underserved areas.
