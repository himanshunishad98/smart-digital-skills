Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Financial Literacy & Wealth Creation Lab

Everything parents ask before enrolling — answered clearly.

📈 Is there real money involved in stock training?
+
No, we use 100% virtual paper trading platforms (like Moneycontrol or Investopedia). This teaches teenagers the mechanics of researching and buying stocks with zero financial risk.
📊 What is the Rule of 72?
+
It is a mathematical shortcut to estimate how long it will take for an investment to double in value at a fixed annual rate of return.
💼 Do you teach tax basics?
+
Yes, we explain income tax, GST, and business expenses conceptually to show teens how businesses operate and how taxes support public services.
👶 Who can join this program?
+
This program is designed for students aged 12–17 years. Students from any school or board (CBSE, State, ICSE) are welcome. No prior experience is required.
✨ Will my child receive a certificate?
+
Yes! Every student who successfully completes the program receives an official SkillNest Certificate of Completion — a great addition to a student's portfolio.
💰 What is the fee? Any installment option?
+
The program fee is . You can reserve your seat for just ₹499 (adjusted in the final fee). You also get a full refund if you're not satisfied after the first class.
📅 What is the batch timing and schedule?
+
Multiple batches are available — morning, afternoon, and weekend batches. Each class is 1 hour long. Weekend batches (Sat & Sun) are available at 10 AM, 3 PM, and 7 PM IST. Weekday batches run Mon–Sat at 9 AM, 2 PM, and 7 PM IST.
🎥 Are the classes recorded? What if my child misses a class?
+
Yes! All online classes are recorded and shared with enrolled students within 24 hours via WhatsApp/Google Drive. Students can watch the recording and catch up anytime.
💸 What is the refund policy?
+
100% Full Refund — After First Class: Attend the very first class. If you or your child is not satisfied for any reason, we will refund the full amount. No questions asked.
