PAA

Parents Also Ask About This Topic

Related queries that parents regularly review.

❓ Which course should my child start with?
+

We recommend selecting based on your child's age group and interests. For ages 6-8, our Foundational Communication & Confidence Lab is the ideal start. For middle schoolers (ages 8-14), Computational Thinking & Coding Logic or Digital Literacy are highly popular. For high schoolers (12-16), AI Literacy, Design Thinking, or Financial Literacy are recommended.

❓ Are these courses suitable for CBSE school syllabus?
+

Yes. The topics align directly with the computer applications goals, 21st-century skills mandates, and digital safety suggestions in the updated CBSE curriculum and NEP 2020 guidelines.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to your top queries regarding computer literacy and program details.

❓ What is the format of SkillNest courses?
+

SkillNest courses are conducted as 100% live, interactive online classes with small batch sizes of 5–10 students, ensuring personalized feedback and hands-on practice.

❓ How long are the courses?
+

Each core cohort runs for 4 to 12 weeks, with flexible weekday and weekend (Saturday and Sunday) sessions designed to fit around school workloads.

❓ What age groups are these courses designed for?
+

The curriculum is built for students aged 6 to 18, with levels tailored to foundational, preparatory, middle, and secondary stages.

❓ Can students access recordings if they miss a class?
+

Yes. All live session recordings are uploaded to the student portal within 24 hours, so students can catch up at their own pace.

❓ Is there a certificate awarded at the end?
+

Yes, every student who completes their course projects earns a verified SkillNest Digital Skill Certificate.

❓ What software is required for these classes?
+

We use standard, free web-based platforms (like Scratch, Canva, Draw.io, Figma) depending on the course requirements.
