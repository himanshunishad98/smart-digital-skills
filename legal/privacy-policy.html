Overview

Your Privacy Matters to Us

SkillNest ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of students, parents, and guardians who interact with our website and educational programs. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

By using our website at SkillNest.co.in or enrolling in any of our programs, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information to deliver our services:

  • ✔ Parent/Guardian Details: Full name, email address, WhatsApp number, and city of residence.
  • ✔ Student Details: First name, class/grade (6, 7, or 8), and age range.
  • ✔ Technical Data: Browser type, device type, and pages visited (via analytics tools).
  • ✔ Communication Data: Messages and queries submitted via our contact forms or WhatsApp.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • ✔ To schedule, confirm, and deliver live class sessions via Zoom.
  • ✔ To send course materials, class links, and progress updates via WhatsApp.
  • ✔ To issue digital certificates upon program completion.
  • ✔ To improve our curriculum and website through analytics insights.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

SkillNest does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for advertising or marketing purposes. We may share limited data only with:

  • ✔ Google Analytics — for anonymized website traffic insights.
  • ✔ Zoom Video Communications — for live class delivery (subject to Zoom's own Privacy Policy).

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience. Cookies are small files stored on your browser. We use session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (for analytics). You may disable cookies via your browser settings, though this may limit some site functionality.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services. Enrolled student data is retained for up to 2 years after program completion for certificate verification purposes. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

  • ✔ Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • ✔ Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • ✔ Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.
  • ✔ Right to Withdraw Consent: Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

7. Contact the Privacy Team
