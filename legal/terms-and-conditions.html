1. Eligibility

SkillNest programs are designed for students in Class 6, 7, or 8 (typically aged 11–14). Enrollment must be completed by a parent or legal guardian. Students under 18 may not independently accept these terms or make payments.

2. Enrollment and Payment

  • ✔ A seat reservation fee secures your batch slot and is adjusted against the full program fee.
  • ✔ Full program fee must be paid before or on the day of the first live session.
  • ✔ All fees are quoted in Indian Rupees (INR) and are subject to applicable taxes.
  • ✔ Please refer to our Refund Policy for complete cancellation and refund terms.

3. Program Delivery

  • ✔ All sessions are conducted live via Zoom. Stable internet and a working device (laptop/desktop preferred) are the student's responsibility.
  • ✔ SkillNest reserves the right to update curriculum content, timings, or instructors with reasonable notice.
  • ✔ Recorded sessions are shared within 24 hours after each class and remain accessible for the program duration.

4. Intellectual Property

All content on the SkillNest website — including curriculum materials, videos, worksheets, lesson plans, and website design — is the exclusive intellectual property of SkillNest. Enrolled students and parents may use materials for personal learning only. Redistribution, resale, or reproduction of any content without written permission is strictly prohibited.

5. Student Code of Conduct

Students are expected to:

  • ✔ Attend sessions punctually and participate actively.
  • ✔ Treat instructors and fellow students with respect.
  • ✔ Not record, share, or distribute live class sessions without written consent.

SkillNest reserves the right to remove a student from the program without refund in cases of serious misconduct.

6. Limitation of Liability

SkillNest's liability is limited to the program fee paid by the parent/guardian. We are not liable for disruptions caused by third-party platforms (Zoom, WhatsApp), internet connectivity issues, or force majeure events. Please also read our Disclaimer for further details.
