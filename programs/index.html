PAA

Parents Also Ask About This Topic

Related queries that parents regularly review.

❓ Which program should my child start with?
+

For absolute beginners in Class 6, we recommend starting with Digital Skills Foundation or touch typing basics. For students with prior computer familiarity, the Coding Program or AI Skills for Students are excellent entry gates.

❓ Is there a verified outcome certificate?
+

Yes. Every student who compiles their digital project portfolio and completes the 6-week syllabus milestones earns a verified SkillNest Program Certificate.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers regarding program details and cbse alignments.

❓ What are the main programs offered by SkillNest?
+

SkillNest offers six core programs: Digital Skills Foundation, AI Skills for Students, Coding Program, Excel for Students, Productivity Tools, and Cyber Safety.

❓ How long does a program cohort last?
+

Each specialized program is structured as a 6-week cohort with live online classes scheduled on weekends or weekday evening slots.

❓ Are classes live or recorded?
+

All sessions are 100% live and interactive with screen sharing and 1-to-1 help. Recording logs are uploaded to the student dashboard within 24 hours.

❓ Is NEP 2020 alignment maintained?
+

Yes, our entire curriculum is mapped to NEP 2020 guidelines for computational thinking, digital safety, and logical design.

❓ What is the batch size for these classes?
+

We maintain small, interactive classes of 5-10 students for personalized attention and feedback.

❓ Can parents monitor learning milestones?
+

Yes, we share weekly WhatsApp trackers and coordinate a live portfolio presentation during the final week of classes.

❓ What software is required?
+

We use free web tools (Scratch, Canva, Google Docs) and standard MS Office applications which do not require paid licenses.

❓ How can I enroll my child in a program node?
+

You can register your child for a free demo session via the primary booking forms on our homepage.
