PAA

Parents Also Ask About This Topic

Related queries that parents regularly review.

❓ What projects do students complete in the program?
+

Students complete projects in word processing (formatting a school report), spreadsheets (an automated marksheet), slide design (a cyber safety presentation), visual coding (a Scratch maze game), and AI prompts.

❓ Are beginner students capable of building these projects?
+

Yes. Our curriculum is tailored for absolute beginners in Class 6–8. Instructors in cities like Raipur and Indore guide students step-by-step from base concepts to complete projects.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to your top queries regarding student outcomes and portfolios.

❓ What is the SkillNest Student Project Gallery?
+

It is a digital outcomes registry showcasing real-world projects in coding, data, presentations, and design created by school students during our cohorts.

❓ Why does SkillNest emphasize project-based computer learning?
+

NEP 2020 guidelines emphasize active computational thinking. Building spreadsheets, documents, and games forces students to apply logic rather than memorizing definitions.

❓ Can students share their portfolios with schools?
+

Yes. Every project file (Excel sheets, Scratch files, PowerPoint decks) belongs to the student and forms a verified portfolio useful for school ICT submissions in Delhi and Mumbai.

❓ How many projects does a student build in the 6-week cohort?
+

Every student builds at least 6 core projects, ending the cohort with a comprehensive digital competency portfolio.

❓ Are the Scratch games playable online?
+

Yes, Scratch games are block-based and can be shared and played online through MIT Scratch project links.

❓ Do students learn to format documents properly?
+

Yes, in the MS Word section, they learn typography rules, line spacing, image anchoring, table structures, and how to build automated tables of contents.

❓ Is safe AI prompting part of the projects?
+

Yes. Under parental supervision, kids learn to document structured prompt queries, learning safe AI boundaries and critical fact-checking processes.

❓ Do parents receive support in regional cities like Bhopal or Indore?
+

Yes. SkillNest supports parents nationwide with live weekend sessions, parent hubs, and clear digital project guides.
